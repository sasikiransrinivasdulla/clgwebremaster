Department Profile

The Department of Basic Science and Humanities started at the inception of the college.The Department is fundamental to Engineering and devoted to fostering the basic principles and understanding of science to enhance the students basic knowledge of Engineering. Its objective is to provide value-based education for budding Scientists and Engineers. The Department offers various courses of study namely Mathematics, Physics ,Chemistry, Environmental Studies, and English. The students benefit greatly from the knowledge and wisdom of Doctors & highly qualified faculty of the department. Faculty make consistent endeavours and strenuous efforts to improve the students' learning and develop their personality.The Department also designs innovative programs which help the students to acquire English Communication Skills and make them industry ready. The Department possesses state-of-the-art laboratories(Sir CV Raman Engineering Physics Lab, Acharya P.C.Ray Engineering Chemistry Lab, and Rabindranath Tagore English Communication Skills Lab) which nurture students practical knowledge through the basic concepts of the courses. Besides this, the department also conducts various scientific and cultural activities like Quizzes, Poster Presentations, Power Point Presentations, Short Films and Skits on National Mathematics Day i.e., on 22nd December, every year to instil students' creativity.
